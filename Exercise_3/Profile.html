Nemecio Bandeira


My name is Nemecio, but I'm known as Nemo by everyone. I'm the main character in the Netflix series "Unauthorized Living". I started smuggling cocaine to the coasts of Galicia when I was 23 years old, and now in my sixties I am the most powerful drug dealer in all Spain. Despite this, I've been recently diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease, so I must look for someone to take my place before I can't recognize even my own children...
